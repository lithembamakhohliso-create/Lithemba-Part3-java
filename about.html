About Easy Event Hire

Your trusted local event equipment partner in Gqeberha

Our Story

Easy Event Hire was founded in Gqeberha (Port Elizabeth) to make quality event equipment accessible to every family and organisation in the Eastern Cape. We believe great events shouldn't break the bank — that's why we offer transparent pricing, reliable delivery, and friendly service every time.

From intimate birthday parties to large community gatherings, we've helped hundreds of events run smoothly. Our equipment is regularly maintained and inspected to ensure everything you hire is in perfect condition.
